xoscope for Linux

xoscope is a digital oscilloscope for Linux!

  • ALSA, ESD, and COMEDI data sources
  • Sweep rates from 2 ns to 2 seconds per division
  • Eight simultaneous display channels
  • Scrollable memory buffers
  • Triggers
  • Cursors
  • Both analog and digital inputs
  • Sweep, accumulate, and strip chart display modes

2015 Feb 11: Version 2.1 is released

Version 2.1 introduces ALSA support (thanks to Gerhard Schiller) and reintroduces the math functions that were removed in the 2.0 release. Bitscope and Probescope support have been permanently dropped.

Download here: xoscope-2.1.tar.gz.
